Player Evaluation

Will Torr is the kind of player you build around — big, strong, fast, and wired to compete. He’s got the ‘it’ factors with a real will to win, works extremely hard, and is a natural leader. At the plate, he profiles as a cleanup hitter with very good speed and a high contact rate — really tough out who can drive both fastballs and breaking stuff out of the yard. He’s aggressive on the bases and smart, which makes him a constant threat once he’s on. Defensively, behind the plate is where he really stands out — already around a 2.1 pop time as an 8th grader, quick release, blocks everything, and controls the game. He can also excel in corner infield and outfield spots. On the mound, he’s still developing a pitching repertoire, but the arm strength and velocity are there with control. A player who positively impacts the game in a lot of ways.

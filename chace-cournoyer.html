Player Evaluation

Chace is a strong, athletic multi-sport guy who really stands out with his work ethic — always the first one there and the last to leave, and it shows in how he plays. He’s a natural center fielder with great instincts, reads the ball quickly off the bat, and covers a ton of ground, making it tough for opponents to find hits in the outfield. He’s got a strong arm and can handle any outfield spot, and his athleticism and solid ground ball mechanics also allow him to step in at second base when needed. At the plate, he’s versatile enough to hit anywhere in the lineup — clean, pro-style swing, drives the ball to the gaps, and maintains a solid contact rate. He can adjust with two strikes, use the whole field, and has the speed to beat out balls. Great opposite field hitter, but will also turn on an inside pitch for a triple.

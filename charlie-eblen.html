Player Evaluation

Charlie’s an elite competitor with discipline that really sets him apart — it shows in his work ethic and how he carries himself on the field. At the plate, he’s fearless with a clean, pro-style swing — a natural 3–5 hole hitter who’s an RBI machine, with power to all fields and a mature, disciplined approach to pitch selection. Defensively, he stands out at third base, making tough forehand and backhand plays with quick feet, a fast release, and a strong, accurate arm. His baseball IQ also allows him to play in the middle infield. On the mound, he competes the same way — attacks hitters with a fastball that acts like a cutter with late movement, making it tough to square up, and mixes in a solid curveball with good control. High-level player who brings intensity and consistency to every part of the game.

Player Evaluation

Dylan’s a really skilled all-around ballplayer who impacts the game in a lot of ways. Defensively, he’s got the glove work of a shortstop, even though he’s played a lot of corner infield — strong arm, handles tough forehand and backhand plays, and does a great job picking and scooping throws at first base. You can move him to the outfield and he’s very effective, with a quick release and an accurate arm that keeps runners honest. At the plate, he shows real power to the pull side against fastballs and has an approach that lets him handle the fireballer, while staying balanced enough to drive off-speed pitches back up the middle. On the mound he really stands out — fastball around 80 mph with a tough-to-pick-up short-arm release, and he mixes in a full arsenal (curve, slider, knuckle, split) with good control, including a sharp split finger that keeps hitters guessing with late, unpredictable movement. Versatile player who can contribute in every phase of the game.

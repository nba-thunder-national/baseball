Player Evaluation

Connor’s best tool is his baseball IQ. He often sees the game a step ahead, understands positioning, pitch sequences, and knows how to win. He’s already 6’1” but still growing into his body, so there’s a lot more upside coming. At the plate, he’s got good hand-eye coordination, keeps his hands inside the ball, and can drive a fastball to all fields while also handling situational hitting very well. For this reason, he puts up good RBI numbers. Defensively, he anchors first base with his length and ability to scoop tough throws, but his footwork, feel, and glove mechanics give him the versatility to play 3B as well, where he shows a quick release off the catch and a strong, accurate arm. On the mound, he’s one of the more advanced pitchers in terms of understanding pitch sequences - he mixes speeds, commands the corners, and keeps hitters off balance with a very good fastball, a split-like changeup, and a well-spotted curve. High-IQ competitor with a lot of projection left.

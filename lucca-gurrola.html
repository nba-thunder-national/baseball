Player Evaluation

Lucca’s one of those players who just finds a way to positively impact the game and winning at a high level. He plays bigger than his size and is a tough out offensively — a high-contact percentage hitter whose approach is built around hard line drives and hard ground balls, rarely giving away at-bats with pop-ups. He comes through in big RBI spots and understands situational hitting, often amongst the team leaders in RBIs. Defensively, he’s versatile and reliable, with good ground ball mechanics in the infield and the ability to take solid angles in the outfield for the tough ones in the gaps and a quick release off the catch — not flashy, but defensively stands out and just consistently gets the job done. On the mound, he commands the zone, mixes speeds well, and keeps hitters off balance with movement and feel. He’s a guy you trust in a bases-loaded, tight game late — not because he overpowers, but because of his command, composure, and mental toughness. He hasn’t hit his growth spurt yet, so there’s a lot more to come. Overall he’s a gritty, highly competitive player who knows how to put the barrel on the ball, make the necessary play, and impact winning.

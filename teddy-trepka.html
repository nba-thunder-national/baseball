Player Evaluation

Teddy’s an elite athlete with all the ‘it’ factors — tough, composed, and a relentless worker. He’s the kind of player teammates look to in big moments because the game never seems too fast for him. At the plate, he’s fearless and built for the middle of the order — quick bat, real power to all fields, plus the speed to impact the game in multiple ways. He’s as clutch as they come when the game’s on the line. Defensively, he’s a standout middle infielder with advanced instincts — reads the ball early, covers a ton of ground, and isn’t afraid of hard contact. He makes tough plays look routine, including throws on the run and from deep in the hole. You can also move him to third or the outfield and not miss a beat. High-level competitor who shows up when it matters most.

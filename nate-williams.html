Player Evaluation

Nate’s the definition of a true utility player — you can put him just about anywhere and feel confident. He’s a standout defensively, whether it’s covering ground in center field or playing the middle infield, where his quick instincts really show. He reads the ball well, fields tough chances to both sides, and can make strong, accurate throws on the run or from deep in the hole. At the plate, he hits with power beyond his size and can fit anywhere in the lineup — disciplined approach, doesn’t chase, and drives the ball to all fields. He’s a tough out who can adjust with two strikes but also do damage when he’s ahead in the count. With his speed and base running IQ, he’s a constant threat on the bases. On the mound, his off-speed stuff really stands out — sharp breaking pitches with late movement and good control, and he uses his fastball effectively to spot and set everything up. High-IQ player who impacts the game in every phase.
